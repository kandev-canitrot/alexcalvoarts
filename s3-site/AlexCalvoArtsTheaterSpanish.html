About Our Classes

Spanish theater classes for adults based on play, listening, and collective creation. Inspired by Jacques Lecoq's pedagogy, we start from the body and movement to discover characters, emotions, and scenes. Here, mistakes don't exist: everything is possibility.

Who Is It For?

For those looking to express themselves, let go, laugh, and connect. You don't need previous experience, just a desire to play, try, and share.

Our Space

A safe, respectful, and judgment-free environment where you can be yourself, be others... or simply be.
