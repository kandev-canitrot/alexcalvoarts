Alexandra Calvo

Actress • Dancer • Teacher

About Me

I was born in Cartagena, and my passion for dance, circus, and physical expression led me to study at R.E.S.A.D. (Royal School of Dramatic Art), where I earned a degree in dramatic arts specializing in "Gestural" under professionals such as Marta Schinca, Jose Piris, Fabio Mangolini, and Ana Vazquez de Castro, among others.

I completed my training with studies in Contemporary Dance with Michelle Man and Jose Reches from 10&10, Oriental Dance at various schools, and Theatrical Improvisation workshops with the Impro-Madrid company. I am constantly training, having completed a master's degree at the Central School of Cinema in recent years, as well as various on-camera seminars.

Professionally, I co-direct a company called "Onira Teatro" with which I have two productions currently running. I have participated in theater works such as "Pingüinas" by Fernando Arrabal, produced by Teatro Español and directed by Juan Carlos Pérez de la Fuente, "Romance de lobos" dir. Jose Piris, "El corral de la Bernarda" dir. Luis Gimenez, "Troppos" dir. Matias Zanotti, "Juicio a los humanos" dir. Jose Piris (currently on tour), and with "No me acuerdo" and "Cardiopatías" directed by Dani Llull.

I have participated in various audiovisual projects and performances as an actress and belly dancer. For more than 7 years, I have been teaching Oriental dance and theater at different schools in Madrid.
